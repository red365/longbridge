Design


At Longbridge Digital we design and build web platforms to the most exacting specifications across a variety of business contexts. From mobile responsive static websites to complex database driven business applications we can deliver you a product that will exceed your expectations.


When building web sites our design philosophy is focused on telling your story, conveying your message with maximum impact. To achieve this we strongly favour minimal yet beautiful design, setting the stage for your branding to be the star!


The web applications that we build are feature rich yet designed with ease of use as the primary factor so your stakeholders are engaged from first use. We also provide training and documentation so you can be productive and efficient from day one using the application.


Our products are built on an open source technology stack so you don't need to worry about expensive licensing costs once your application has been rolled out. We will work with you to help you identify the best hosting provider for the nature of the application and perform the roll-out and initial configuration to get you up and running!


To discuss your requirements with us please email design@longbridgedigital.co.uk

SEO (Search Engine Optimisation)


Are potential clients having trouble finding you on the web? Search Engine Optimisation is the art of driving new business to your web site by making you more visible to potential clients who are using a search engine to find businesses in your sector.


We utilise a range of tools and SEO strategies to discover what keywords your potential clients are searching for, what keywords your competitors are using and from there devise a plan to rank above them in search engine results.


If you think your search engine ranking could do with a boost please email seo@longbridgedigital.co.uk


Analytics


Are you struggling to convert site visitors into members or paying customers? We specialise in analysing site metrics for our clients, tracking user journeys through your web site or application and discovering pain points where your stakeholders may be having difficulty with the site or losing their sense of engagement with your message.


We can help you fix these issues and ensure you build lasting relationships with visitors to your site. We can also design and build dashboards to highlight key metrics and make analysing the behaviour of your site traffic a piece of cake!
